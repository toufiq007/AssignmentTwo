Lionel Messi wins his first international trophy after 15 years

Messi has won his first international trophy after making his debut at this stage in 2006.This was his 10th senior international tournament.World Cup: 2006 (quarter-final), 2010 (quarter-final), 2014 (runners-up), 2018 (last 16).Copa America: 2007 (runners-up), 2011 (quarter-final), 2015 (runners-up), 2016 (runners-up), 2019 (semi-final), 2021 (winners).Notably, Messi played his 34th match at Copa America (19 at World Cup).

Italy won its first title in 53 years England beat England on penalties at Euro 2020

Italian players celebrate their trophies after winning the Euro 2020 European Football Championship (Euro 2020) held at Wembley Stadium in London, England, on the 11th (local time). In the final, Italy went on a penalty shoot-out against England 1-1 in 120 minutes until extra time, beating England 3-2 to win the championship. This is the first European football championship in Italy since the 1968 tournament was held in their home country
